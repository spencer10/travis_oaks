Improvements at S. Congress and Oltorf now complete!

POSTED August 9, 2018

South Congress Avenue and Oltorf Street is a safer intersection to ride, walk and drive through now that the Austin Transportation Department has made some changes there. The department has photos of the improved intersection here, and a map of the improvements here, which include raised medians, bicycle detection and signals, restriped crosswalks, ADA-accessible ramps and more. This is just one of several planned Vision Zero Intersection Safety projects that were funded through the 2016 mobility bond. The department held a press conference at the intersection July 31st at 9 a.m., and the Vision Zero street team was at the nearby HEB parking lot at that time to give out complimentary safety gear such as bike lights, reflective wristbands and backpack clips.
